# Auditor's Independence Declaration Template

All members of an audit team must be independent of the audit client. Use this standard declaration form at the start of every audit engagement to identify potential threats to independence as per the **ICAN Code of Ethics 2023**.

---

**To:** The Engagement Partner, [Your Firm Name]
**From:** [Your Name]
**Re:** Independence Declaration for Audit of [Client Name]
**Date:** [DD/MM/YYYY]

---

I confirm that I have read and understood the **ICAN Code of Ethics** regarding independence. To the best of my knowledge and belief, I declare the following:

### 1. Financial Interests
- [ ] I do NOT hold any direct financial interest (shares, debentures) in the above client.
- [ ] My immediate family members do NOT hold any material financial interest in the above client.

### 2. Loans and Guarantees
- [ ] I have NOT accepted any loan or guarantee from the client (unless it is a bank under normal lending procedures).
- [ ] I do NOT have any outstanding fees due from previous engagements with this client that could be considered a loan.

### 3. Business Relationships
- [ ] I do NOT have a close business relationship with the client or its management (e.g., joint venture, marketing arrangement).

### 4. Employment Relationships
- [ ] I have NOT served as a Director, Officer, or Employee of the client during the period covered by the financial statements.
- [ ] I do NOT have any intention of joining the client as an employee in the near future.

### 5. Family and Personal Relationships
- [ ] No immediate family member of mine is a Director, Officer, or employee in a position to exert significant influence over the subject matter of the engagement.

### 6. Gifts and Hospitality
- [ ] I have NOT accepted gifts or hospitality from the client, unless the value is trivial and inconsequential.

---

### Disclosure of Potential Threats

If any of the above statements are NOT true, or if there are any other circumstances that might threaten my independence (e.g., long association with client personnel), please detail them below:

**[ ]** I have a potential threat to disclose:
_________________________________________________________________________
(Description of threat, e.g., "My cousin is an accounts officer at the client firm.")

---

**Signature:** _________________________
**Name:** [Your Name]
**Designation:** [Partner / Manager / Audit Assistant]

---

::: tip Compliance Note
This declaration should be renewed annually for recurring audit clients.
