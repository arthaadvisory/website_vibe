# Employment Contract Template

*A standard draft agreement compliant with the **Labor Act 2074** and **Labor Rules 2075**.*

## Key Clauses Included

### 1. Appointment & Tenure
*   **Probation Period:** Strictly 6 months (as per Labor Act).
*   **Job Type:** Regular / Time-Bound / Work-Based.

### 2. Remuneration (The "Basic Salary" Rule)
*   **Basic Salary:** Must be at least ~60% of total gross (to ensure proper SSF contribution).
*   **Dearness Allowance:** Fixed allowance adjusted for inflation.
*   **SSF Deduction:** Explicitly mentioning the 11% (Employee) and 20% (Employer) contribution.

### 3. Leave & Holidays
*   **Annual Leave:** 1 day for every 20 days worked.
*   **Sick Leave:** Fully paid (up to 12 days/year).
*   **Mourning Leave:** 13 days.

### 4. Termination
*   **Notice Period:** 1 month (or salary in lieu of notice).
*   **Severance:** Gratuity and Leave Encashment payable upon exit.
