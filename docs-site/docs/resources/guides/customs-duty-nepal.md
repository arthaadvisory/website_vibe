# Customs Duty Rates in Nepal (FY 2081/82)

*Know the landed cost of your imports before they ship.*

<ReadTime />

Importing goods into Nepal attracts primarily three types of taxes at the customs point:
1.  **Customs Duty**: Based on HS Code.
2.  **Excise Duty**: On luxury or health-hazard goods.
3.  **VAT**: Flat 13% on (Customs Value + Duty + Excise).

### Petroleum Vehicles
*   **Motorcycles (>250cc)**: 80% Customs + 60% Excise.
*   **Cars (>1000cc)**: 80% Customs + 60% Excise (Base).

## ü™ô Gold & Silver

*   **Gold (Raw)**: NPR 20,000 per 10 grams.
*   **Silver**: NPR 107 per 10 grams.

> **Limit:** Individuals traveling from abroad can bring up to **50 grams** of gold jewelry (tax-free) and an additional **100 grams** by paying full duty. Raw gold bars are strictly prohibited for individuals.
