# Doing Business in Nepal: A Guide for Investors (2025)

Nepal offers a strategic location between India and China, competitive labor costs, and a government increasingly open to Foreign Direct Investment (FDI). This guide provides the tactical roadmap to entering the Nepali market.

## 3. Taxation at a Glance

*   **Corporate Tax:** 25% (Standard) / 20% (Manufacturing/Special) / 30% (Banks).
*   **VAT:** 13% Single Rate.
*   **Dividends:** 5% Final Withholding Tax (for foreign companies).
*   **Repatriation Tax:** 0% (Post-dividend tax payment).

## 4. Labor Laws

*   **Minimum Wage:** NPR 17,300 per month (Basic + Dearness Allowance) *[Subject to periodic revision]*.
*   **Social Security:** Mandatory.
    *   Employer Contribution: 20%
    *   Employee Contribution: 11%
*   **Hiring Expats:** Allowed up to 5% of total workforce (or 3 experts) if local talent is unavailable. Requires Work Permit.

## 5. Incentives for Investors

*   **Hydropower:** 100% Tax Holiday for first 10 years + 50% for next 5 years.
*   **Manufacturing:** Duties on machinery import as low as 1%.
*   **IT/Exports:** Income from software export taxed at only 1%.
