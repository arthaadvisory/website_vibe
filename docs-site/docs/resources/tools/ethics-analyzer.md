## About this Tool
This analyzer is designed for Chartered Accountants and professional firms in Nepal following the **Code of Ethics** issued by the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nepal (ICAN), which is based on the IESBA Handbook.

### Common Threat Categories
1. **Self-Interest Threat**: Financial or other interests influencing judgment.
2. **Self-Review Threat**: Evaluating one's own previous work.
3. **Advocacy Threat**: Promoting a client's position to the point of compromising objectivity.
4. **Familiarity Threat**: Long or close relationships leading to over-sympathetic behavior.
5. **Intimidation Threat**: Deterrence from acting objectively due to pressure.
