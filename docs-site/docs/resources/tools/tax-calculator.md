### Allowable Deductions
*   **Life Insurance:** Max NPR 40,000.
*   **Medical Insurance:** Max NPR 20,000.
*   **CIT / Provident Fund:** 1/3rd of total income or NPR 300,000 (whichever is lower).
*   **Remote Area Allowance:** Deductible based on category (A to E).
