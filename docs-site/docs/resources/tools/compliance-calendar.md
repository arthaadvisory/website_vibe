## ðŸ“¢ Annual Compliance Deadlines

### **Office of Company Registrar (OCR)**
*   **AGM (Annual General Meeting):** Within 6 months of FY end (Poush End).
*   **Annual Return (Dafa 78):** Within 30 days of AGM.
*   **Share Lagat (Dafa 51):** Along with Annual Return.

### **Inland Revenue Department (IRD)**
*   **Income Tax Return (D-01/D-02/D-03):** Initial deadline is Ashwin End. Can be extended for 3 months (Poush End) with written request.
*   **Tax Clearance Certificate:** Apply immediately after filing return.

### **Department of Industry (DOI)**
*   **Annual Progress Report:** Within 3 months of FY end.
*   **Audit Report Submission:** Within 6 months of FY end.

---

> **Tip:** Late filing of VAT attracts a penalty of **NPR 1,000 per return** or **0.05% per day**, whichever is higher. Don't risk it.
