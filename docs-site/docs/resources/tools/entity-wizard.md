## Comparison of Common Entities

| Feature | Private Limited | Sole Proprietorship | Partnership |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Liability** | Limited | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| **Taxation** | Corporate Tax | Individual Slab | Individual Slab |
| **Registration** | Office of Company Registrar | Department of Commerce/Industry | Department of Commerce |
| **Continuity** | Perpetual | Ends with Owner | Depends on Agreement |

> [!NOTE]
> Foreign investors generally must incorporate as a **Private Limited Company** or register a **Branch/Liaison Office**.
