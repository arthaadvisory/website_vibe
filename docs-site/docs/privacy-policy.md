**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024

At **Artha Advisory**, we are committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of the personal information of our clients, partners, and website visitors. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclosure, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or engage our services.

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:
*   Contact us via our website forms.
*   Subscribe to our newsletters or alerts.
*   Apply for a job or internship.
*   Register for client portals or events.

This information may include your name, email address, phone number, company name, and any other details you choose to provide.

## 2. Web Server Logs & Cookies

Like most websites, we automatically collect certain non-personal information when you visit our site. This may include:
*   **IP Address:** To diagnose server problems and administer the site.
*   **Browser Type & Version:** To ensure compatibility and optimal display.
*   **Cookies:** Small text files stored on your device to enhance user experience (e.g., remembering preferences). You can control cookie settings through your browser.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:
*   **Service Delivery:** To respond to your inquiries and provide the consulting services you request.
*   **Communication:** To send you technical updates, newsletters, and invitations to seminars (only if you have opted in).
*   **Recruitment:** To process job applications and assess suitability.
*   **Legal Compliance:** To comply with applicable laws and regulatory requirements in Nepal (e.g., KYC norms).

## 4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## 5. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., Inland Revenue Department, Nepal Rastra Bank). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

## 6. Access and Correction

You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you and to ask for corrections if it is inaccurate. To exercise this right, please contact our Information Officer.
