## üè≠ Operational Excellence

### 1. Cost Audit & Efficiency
In a competitive market, margin management is critical.
*   **Cost Audit**: Analyzing the "Cost of Production" per unit to identify wastage and improve gross margins.
*   **Yield Analysis**: Verifying input-output ratios (Raw Material to Finished Goods) to detect pilferage.
*   **Overhead Allocation**: Ensuring factory overheads are correctly absorbed into product costing.

### 2. Supply Chain & Inventory
Inventory is often a manufacturer's largest asset‚Äîand biggest risk.
*   **Perpetual Inventory System**: Implementing barcode-based tracking for real-time stock visibility.
*   **Stock Audit**: Surprise physical verification of high-value raw materials and WIP.
*   **Vendor Audit**: Reviewing procurement contracts to ensure you aren't overpaying for supplies.

### 3. Excise & Customs Advisory
*   **Excise Duty**: Precision in calculating duty on ex-factory price and managing EX-IM codes.
*   **Bonded Warehouse**: Setting up duty-free import mechanisms for raw materials used in export-oriented goods.
*   **Duty Drawback**: Assisting exporters in claiming refunds on import duties paid.
