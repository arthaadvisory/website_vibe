The trading and retail sector is at the heart of Nepal's economy. Artha Advisory provides robust financial governance and tax advisory services to wholesalers, retailers, and FMCG distributors to ensure they stay profitable in a high-volume, low-margin environment.

## Profitability & Control for Traders

### 1. Robust Inventory & Stock Management
Managing thousands of SKUs (Stock Keeping Units) requires precision. We provide:
- **Stock Audits**: Independent physical verification of inventory across multiple warehouse locations.
- **Shrinkage Analysis**: Identifying and mitigating inventory losses due to theft, damage, or accounting errors.
- **Inventory Valuation**: Ensuring compliance with NAS 2 and accurate calculation of Year-End stock values.

### 2. VAT, Excise & Customs Management
Trading businesses are highly sensitive to indirect tax efficiency:
- **VAT Reconciliation**: Matching purchase and sales invoices to ensure correct VAT credit claims and preventing IRD mismatch notices.
- **Excise Duty Reporting**: Managing the complex calculation and reporting for items subject to excise duty at the point of import or manufacture.
- **Post-Clearance Audit (PCA) Support**: Assisting businesses in responding to customs-related audits after the goods have entered the market.

### 3. Margin Analysis & Financial Governance
Helping traders understand their real profitability:
- **Category Profitability Review**: Analyzing gross and net margins across different product categories to optimize inventory mix.
- **Cash Flow Management**: Strategic planning for working capital, especially for seasonal businesses.
- **Internal Controls over Cash**: Implementing secure cash-handling and reconciliation processes for retail outlets.

## Specialized Services for Retailers

- **POS System Auditing**: Verifying that Point-of-Sale data matches financial records and bank deposits.
- **Distributor Financial Advisory**: Assisting distributors in managing credit risk and vendor relationships.
- **Franchise Compliance**: Auditing royalty payments and financial obligations for local and international franchises.
