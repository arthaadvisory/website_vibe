Artha Advisory provides comprehensive audit, tax, and advisory services specifically tailored for Banks and Financial Institutions (BFIs) in Nepal. From Class 'A' Commercial Banks to Savings & Credit Cooperatives, we ensure financial stability and regulatory compliance.

## üè¶ Core BFI Services (Class A, B, C, D)

### 1. Regulatory Compliance (NRB)
*   **Directives Compliance**: Auditing adherence to the *Unified Directives* issued by Nepal Rastra Bank (NRB).
*   **Capital Adequacy (CAR)**: Calculation of Risk-Weighted Assets (RWA) and Tier 1/Tier 2 capital as per Basel III.
*   **Returns Filing**: Reviewing SIS (Supervisory Information System) returns before submission.

### 2. NFRS Implementation & ECL
*   **NFRS 9 Advisory**: Developing models for Expected Credit Loss (ECL) calculation‚Äîmoving from rule-based to principle-based provisioning.
*   **Actuarial Valuation**: Valuing employee benefits (Leave, Gratuity) as per NAS 19.

### 3. Risk-Based Internal Audit (RBIA)
*   **Credit Audit**: Granular review of loan files, collateral valuation, and borrower creditworthiness.
*   **Treasury Audit**: Verifying forex positions, inter-bank lending, and investment portfolio valuation.
*   **AML/CFT Audit**: Independent testing of Anti-Money Laundering controls and KYC procedures.
