### 1. Social Welfare Council (SWC)
*   **Affiliation & Renewal**: Guiding new NGOs through SWC affiliation and periodic renewals.
*   **Project Approval**: Assisting in the financial budgeting for General Agreements (GA) and Project Agreements (PA).
*   **Project Audit**: Conducting the mandatory project completion audit required by SWC.

## üåç Donor Compliance & Reporting

### 1. Grant Management
*   **Fund Accounting**: Setting up systems to track Restricted vs. Unrestricted funds separately.
*   **Donor-Specific Reporting**: Preparing financial reports in specific formats (USAID SF-425, EU Financial Statement, UN FACE Forms).
*   **Overhead Negotiation**: Justifying administrative overhead allocation (NICRA) to donors.

### 2. Social Audit & Impact
*   **Social Audit**: Facilitating the mandatory public hearing and social audit process at the local ward level.
*   **Value for Money (VfM)**: Analyzing the economy, efficiency, and effectiveness of program spends.
