From seed funding to stock exchange listing, **Artha Advisory** provides the technical backbone for Nepal's growing capital markets. We bridge the gap between regulatory requirements and commercial ambition.

## For Private Equity & VC Funds

*   **Fund Registration:** Drafting the Fund Deed and handling AIF license application with SEBON.
*   **Fund Management Company (FMC) Setup:** Incorporation and compliance.
*   **Deal Structuring:** Advising on equity vs. convertible debt instruments for portfolio investments.
*   **Due Diligence:** Conducting "Red Flag" financial and tax diligence on startups before you invest.
*   **Exit Strategy:** Tax planning for IPO exits or secondary sales.

## For Stock Brokers & Merchant Bankers

Compliance in this high-frequency trading environment must be watertight.

### **Our Services:**
*   **Semi-Annual Audits:** Mandatory regulatory audits for SEBON compliance.
*   **Net Worth Certification:** Periodic certification of Capital Adequacy.
*   **AML/CFT Compliance:** Ensuring KYC norms are met for all brokerage clients to prevent money laundering.
*   **System Audit:** Verifying the integrity of the Order Management System (TMS).

## For Companies Planning an IPO

Going public is a marathon, not a sprint. We act as your "IPO Readiness" partner.
*   **Conversion to Public Ltd:** Amending the MOA/AOA.
*   **Financial Restructuring:** Cleaning up the balance sheet to remove non-compliant assets.
*   **Prospectus Financials:** preparing the 3-year historical financial track record required for the prospectus.
*   **Book Building Support:** Assisting in the valuation logic for premium price issuance.
