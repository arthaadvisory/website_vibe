The real estate and construction industry in Nepal faces unique challenges ranging from complex land acquisition laws to specialized tax treatment for long-term projects. **Artha Advisory** provides end-to-end financial and legal support for developers and investors.

## Core Competencies

### 1. Project Costing & Inventory Valuation
Accurate costing is critical for project-based accounting. We help developers:
- **WIP Valuation**: Calculating the value of Work-in-Progress (WIP) as per NAS 11 / NFRS 15.
- **Material Leakage Audit**: Physical verification of construction materials and inventory tracking.

### 2. Tax Structuring & VAT Compliance
- **Project-Specific Tax Planning**: Advising on the most efficient entity structure for SPVs (Special Purpose Vehicles).
- **VAT on Real Estate**: Navigating the complex VAT rules regarding residential vs. commercial property sales.
- **Reverse Charge VAT**: Auditing payments made to foreign contractors and consultants.

### 3. AML/CFT & Regulatory Compliance
- **Directives 2082**: Implementing KYC and Due Diligence for "Designated Non-Financial Businesses and Professions" (DNFBPs) in the real estate sector.
- **Social Responsibility**: Assisting in compliance with local environmental and social safeguards (IEE/EIA) for large developments.

### 4. Feasibility & Funding Advisory
- **Real Estate Feasibility**: Financial modeling for hotels, shopping malls, and housing projects.
- **Loan Syndication**: Preparing Project Reports and CMA data for bank financing.
