Nepal's tech sector is rapidly evolving, with new digital services and global outsourcing models. Artha Advisory provides forward-thinking financial and tax advice to software companies, IT exporters, and tech startups.

## Financial Strategy for Innovation

### 1. Digital Service Tax (DST) & International Tax
The introduction of **Digital Service Tax** in Nepal has significant implications for global tech providers. We provide:
- **DST Compliance**: Helping companies navigate the registration, calculation, and filing of Digital Service Tax.
- **Export Incentive Advisory**: Assisting IT exporters (BPO, Software Development) in claiming mandatory tax exemptions and foreign currency incentives.
- **Transfer Pricing for IT**: Ensuring that inter-company transactions between global branches are compliant with local transfer pricing laws.

### 2. Startup Advisory & Valuation
Supporting the next generation of Nepali entrepreneurs:
- **Business Valuation**: Providing professional valuation services for startups looking for seed or venture capital funding.
- **ESOP Design**: Structuring Employee Stock Option Plans that align with Nepali labor and tax laws.
- **Pitch-Deck Financials**: Validating financial projections to ensure they are robust and investor-ready.

### 3. Modern Accounting Systems
Tech companies need agile financial reporting:
- **Cloud Accounting Setup**: Implementing modern, cloud-based accounting solutions (like Xero or QuickBooks) tailored for tech workflows.
- **SaaS Metrics Auditing**: Reviewing specialized metrics like MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue), Churn Rate, and CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost).
- **R&D Tax Credits**: Identifying opportunities for tax deductions related to Research and Development.

## Key Compliance Services

- **IP & Licensing Compliance**: Auditing the financial management of Intellectual Property and software licenses.
- **Revenue Recognition (NFRS 15)**: implementing complex revenue recognition models for multi-year software contracts and subscriptions.
- **Data Privacy Financial Impact**: Reviewing the financial risks associated with data protection and privacy compliance.
