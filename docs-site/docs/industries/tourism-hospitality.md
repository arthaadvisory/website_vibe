From boutique hotels in Kathmandu to luxury resorts in the Himalayas, **Artha Advisory** provides the financial clarity required to manage seasonality and regulatory shifts in the tourism sector.

## Sector-Specific Services

### 1. Hospitality Tax Planning
Optimizing tax liabilities for hotels and resorts, including VAT reconciliation, service charge audits, and corporate tax structuring.

### 2. Project Feasibility & Modeling
Financial modeling and feasibility studies for new hotel projects and resort developments, assisting in securement of debt and equity funding.

### 3. TDS & VAT Compliance
Managing the unique tax withholding requirements for foreign travel bookings, agency commissions, and international marketing expenses.

### 4. Internal Controls
Designing and auditing internal control systems for cash-intensive hospitality operations to prevent leakage and ensure transparency.
