## üè¢ Company Registration & Startups

<Accordion title="What is the minimum capital required to register a private limited company?">
Currently, there is no minimum paid-up capital requirement for registering a private limited company in Nepal. However, you must declare an authorized capital amount. For foreign investment (FDI), the minimum threshold is **NPR 20 Million** per investor.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How long does it take to register a company?">
Once all documents (online submission via OCR) are in order, the Company Registrar's Office (OCR) typically approves registration within **3-7 working days**. Post-registration, you must obtain a PAN/VAT certificate from the Inland Revenue Department.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can a foreigner own 100% of a company in Nepal?">
Yes, for most sectors (Software, Manufacturing, Tourism, Energy), **100% foreign ownership is allowed**. Certain sectors like Primary Agriculture, Media, and Real Estate Brokerage are restricted (Negative List).
</Accordion>

## üí∞ Taxation & VAT

<Accordion title="When do I need to register for VAT?">
Mandatory VAT registration applies if your annual turnover exceeds:
*   **NPR 5 Million** for goods.
*   **NPR 2 Million** for services.
*   **NPR 2 Million** for mixed (goods + services).
Certain businesses (e.g., Hardware, Liquor, Consulting) may need to register regardless of turnover.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What is the corporate tax rate in Nepal?">
The standard rate is **25%**. However, special rates apply:
*   **Banks & Financial Institutions:** 30%
*   **Manufacturing Industries:** 20% (with further rebates for export/employment).
*   **Special Industries (Hydro, IT):** Concessional rates or holidays may apply under Section 11.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Does Nepal have a Capital Gains Tax (CGT)?">
Yes.
*   **Individuals:** 5% or 7.5% on shares (depending on holding period); 5% or 7.5% on land/building.
*   **Entities:** 10% on shares; 1.5% on land/building (as advance tax), subject to final audit.
</Accordion>

## üìä Audit & Compliance

<Accordion title="Is a statutory audit mandatory for all companies?">
Yes, every registered company (Private or Public) must appoint an auditor and submit an audited financial report to the OCR and IRD annually, regardless of profit or loss status.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What is the deadline for filing tax returns?">
*   **D-01/D-02/D-03 (Income Tax):** Within 3 months of the fiscal year-end (by **Ashwin end**). This can be extended by 3 months upon request.
*   **VAT Returns:** Monthly (or Trimester for some), by the 25th of the following month.
</Accordion>
