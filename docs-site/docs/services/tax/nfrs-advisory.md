Nepal's transition to international accounting standards demands professional precision. **Artha Advisory** helps companies navigate the complexity of NFRS implementation, ensuring your financial reporting is transparent, credible, and compliant with global best practices.

## The NFRS Framework in Nepal

| Framework | Target Group |
|---|---|
| Banks, Insurance, Listed Companies | **Full NFRS** |
| Large unlisted companies | **Full NFRS** |
| SMEs (below threshold) | **NFRS for SMEs** |
| Micro entities | **NAS (Nepal Accounting Standards)** |

## Our Advisory Solutions

### 1. NFRS Transition & Gap Analysis
Reviewing your current accounting policies and identifying the necessary adjustments required to align with NFRS standards.

### 2. First-Time Adoption (NFRS 1)
Managing the complex "Day 1" adjustments needed when a company moves from legacy standards to NFRS for the first time.

### 3. Technical Paper Writing
Drafting the required accounting technical papers for your auditors to justify the valuation methods and assumptions used in your financial reports.

## Why NFRS Matters?
*   **Transparency**: Provides a clearer picture of financial health to stakeholders and investors.
*   **Comparability**: Allows your business to be benchmarked against international peers.
*   **Creditability**: Increases confidence for banks and foreign investors looking to fund your growth.
