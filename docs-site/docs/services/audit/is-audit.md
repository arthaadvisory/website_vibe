## Why Do You Need an IS Audit?

1.  **Regulatory Mandate:**
    *   **BFIs:** All Class A, B, and C banks must conduct an annual IS Audit as per the NRB Unified Directive.
    *   **Fintech:** Digital wallets and gateways are subject to periodic security reviews for licensing.
2.  **Customer Trust:** Certification assures your users that their financial data and transactions are safe.
3.  **Risk Mitigation:** Proactively identify vulnerabilities from external threats and internal access risks.

## Our IS Audit Services

### 1. IT General Controls (ITGC) Review
We assess the foundational controls of your IT environment:
*   Access Control & User Management.
*   Change Management Procedures for software updates.
*   Backup & Disaster Recovery Drills and documentation.
*   Physical Security of Data Centers/Server Rooms.

### 2. Application Control Review
Deep dive into your core banking system (CBS) or ERP:
*   Input/Output Validation and Integrity.
*   Interest Calculation Logic and financial accruals.
*   Segregation of Duties (SoD) within the software environment.

### 3. Vulnerability Assessment
We collaborate with certified ethical hackers to identify potential security gaps in your network and web applications (OWASP Top 10 vulnerabilities).

### 4. NRB Compliance Reporting
We map your controls directly to the **NRB IT Guidelines** and produce a gap analysis report acceptable to the central bank's inspection team.

## Our Methodology: COBIT & ISO 27001

Our audit approach is aligned with global best practices:
1.  **Plan:** Understand business processes and IT scope.
2.  **Assess:** Test controls using automated tools and manual review.
3.  **Report:** Categorize risks as High/Medium/Low with actionable remediation steps.
4.  **Follow-up:** Verify that critical gaps are closed before the next regulatory inspection.
