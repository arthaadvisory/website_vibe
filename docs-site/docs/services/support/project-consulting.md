High-stakes projects require more than just standard accounting; they need industry-specific technical advisory. **Artha Advisory** provides specialized support for infrastructure developers, hydropower companies, and humanitarian agencies, ensuring that your large-scale investments are protected and compliant.

## Sector-Specific Advisory

### 1. Energy & Hydropower
Assistance through the complex lifecycle of power projects:
- **Financial Closure**: Preparing bank-ready feasibility reports and financial models.
- **Tax Incentives**: Leveraging the "Income Tax Holiday" for energy producers.
- **Regulatory Filings**: Liaison with the **Electricity Regulatory Commission (ERC)**.

### 2. Infrastructure & Construction
- **Joint Venture (JV) Structuring**: Managing the trilateral relationship between local partners, foreign contractors, and tax authorities.
- **Withholding Tax (TDS)**: Ensuring correct tax retention on large-scale payments to sub-contractors.

### 3. NGO/INGO Support
Specialized financial management in compliance with the **Social Welfare Council (SWC)**:
- **Project Audits**: Grant-specific financial reporting for international donors.
- **SWC Approvals**: Support through the Project Agreement (PA) and General Agreement (GA) process.

## Core Consulting Solutions

### 1. Monitoring & Evaluation (M&E)
Designing logical frameworks and results-chains to track project impact for infrastructure developers and donor-funded programs.

### 2. Contract Management Advisory
Vetting and review of procurement and construction contracts to identify financial risks and ensure fair payment terms.

### 3. Financial Risk & Feasibility
Ensuring multi-year projects have sustainable cash flow structures and identifying potential budget overruns early in the execution cycle.
